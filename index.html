Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 30, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how AndroFrenzy (“we”, “us”, or “our”) handles information across all our mobile applications (“Apps”).

Overview

We do not collect, store, track, sell, or share personal data. All app data (such as progress, favorites, and settings) remains on the user’s device and is not transmitted to our servers or third parties.

Data We Do Not Collect

  • No names, email addresses, phone numbers, or account details.
  • No precise or approximate location data.
  • No device identifiers, analytics, or usage statistics.
  • No access to contacts, photos, media, files, camera, microphone, or sensors.
  • No cookies or similar tracking technologies.

Because we do not collect data, we do not sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties.

On‑Device Storage Only

Any app content, preferences, or study progress are stored locally on the device. Removing the app (or using an in‑app “Clear Data” option, where available) deletes the locally stored data.

Advertising and Third‑Party SDKs

No ads are currently enabled in our Apps. If advertising or third‑party SDKs are added in the future, we will update this policy before release to identify the partners used, what data (if any) they process, for what purposes (e.g., analytics, advertising), and provide consent/opt‑out mechanisms where required by law and platform policies.

Children’s Privacy

Our Apps are suitable for general audiences and do not collect personal data. If future features involve third‑party services or data processing subject to child‑specific regulations, we will implement appropriate safeguards and update this policy accordingly.

User Rights and Controls

  • Access and Deletion: We hold no server‑side personal data. Local app data can be cleared via in‑app controls (if available) or by uninstalling the app.
  • Permissions: Our Apps do not request sensitive permissions. If any future feature requires permissions, it will be requested at runtime with a clear explanation of purpose.

Security

Since we do not collect or transmit personal data, related risks are minimized. If future versions introduce network features or third‑party services, we will apply appropriate technical and organizational safeguards and update this policy.

International Data Transfers

No personal data is collected or transferred internationally. If future changes introduce data processing, we will disclose any transfers and applicable safeguards where required.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect new features, legal requirements, or platform policies. The “Last updated” date will be revised and, where required, additional notice or consent will be provided.
